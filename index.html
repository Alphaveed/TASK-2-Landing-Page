ABOUT US



The DIAMOND CLUB was founded in 2019 in India. It is a non-profit organization in the world formed specifically to represent the main stakeholders in the nightlife economy, including restaurants, bars, clubs, discos, lounges and other providers linked to the nightlife sector.We aim to create an environment of complete trust, where customers, their families, authorities, and all participants can have full confidence in nightlife venues that display our distinctive marks.
